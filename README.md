Kakeibo is a traditional Japanese budgeting method focused on mindful spending and intentional saving.
Instead of relying on apps or automation, it encourages reflection before spending money.

## What is Kakeibo?

Kakeibo originated in Japan in the early 20th century.
It uses simple pen-and-paper budgeting to help people become more aware of their financial habits.

## Core Principles of the Kakeibo Method

- Awareness before spending
- Monthly reflection instead of daily tracking
- Behaviour change over automation
- Simple and intentional money management

## Who is Kakeibo Best For?

Kakeibo is useful for people who:
- Feel overwhelmed by budgeting apps
- Want to reduce impulsive spending
- Prefer mindful and simple systems
- Want long-term money habits
